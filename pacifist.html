TRUE PACIFIST Timeline

In the TRUE PACIFIST Timeline, you cannot kill anything. If you do, you are unable to complete the timeline. Throughout the game, you also have to go on dates with certain characters. Many people have also called this route the cannon/true ending to UNDERTALE.


Since the Neutral Timeline still occurs during the Pacifist Run, we have included the Neutral timeline. There is some extra information on how to complete the route correctly. If you would like to skip the Neurtal Timeline, please click this button.


RUINS

When the HUMAN wakes up, they are greeted to Flowey the Flower. There, the HUMAN also encounters their first fight. A theme with Flowey is "Kill or be killed." When the HUMAN is at 1 HP, Toriel saves them by attacking Flowey. Toriel then shows the HUMAN around, acting as a caregiver to them. Toriel is also the first main battle that the HUMAN must fight.

SNOWDIN

Once the HUMAN leaves the RUINS, they then go into SNOWDIN town, home to Sans and Papyrus. Here, the HUMAN completes many puzzles for Papyrus. They are all relatively simple, so Papyrus decides to challenge the HUMAN by attacking them, making him the second boss fight the HUMAN encounters. After Papyrus is spared, he decides to take the HUMAN on a date in his room. This is an important step to complete the route.

WATERFALL

After SNOWDIN, the HUMAN passes by WATERFALL, where they run away from Undyne, the Royal Guarde in UNDERTALE whose goal is to kill a HUMAN to get their soul for Asgore. If the HUMAN decides to spare Undyne, she decides to not attack them anymore. After the HUMAN battles Undyne, they go to her house per Papyrus's request to make amends. Undyne decides to bake pasta with the HUMAN, which ends up burning her house down. This is an important step to complete the route.

HOTLAND

When entering HOTLAND, the HUMAN meets Alphys in her lab. The HUMAN learns that Alphys has been stalking them the entire time, watching their every move. The HUMAN also meets Mettaton, a robot who is thristy for a HUMAN soul. While going through HOTLAND, the HUMAN has to complete various puzzles that Mettaton has set up.

MTT Resort

MTT RESTORT is where the HUMAN learns that Mettaton is a beloved figure in the UNDERGROUND who has a TV show dedicated to game shows. This is also where the HUMAN fights Mettaton. No matter what the HUMAN does, Mettaton will always explode.

TRUE HOME

Walking deeper into the UNDERGROUND, the HUMAN finds the TRUE HOME, which is an identical replica of the RUINS, just in black and white. This is where the HUMAN learns from all the monsters that they have met that Toriel and Asgore were married, and they had a child together. One day, a HUMAN fell into the underground, whom they took in as their own. The HUMAN fell ill one day, and Asriel decided to bring their body back up as it was their final wish. However, the humans above thought Asriel killed the HUMAN, so they killed Asriel. From then on, Asgore decided that any human that falls down must be killed. Therefore, Toriel divorced him. The TRUE HOME was their first home.

JUDGEMENT HALL

In the JUDGEMENT HALL, the HUMAN meets Sans again, but this time, he's more serious. Here, the HUMAN is judged for everything they have done. Since the HUMAN hasn't killed anyone, Sans praises them, saying that they are close to the end of the game.

THRONE ROOM

This is the last room the HUMAN goes through. Here, the HUMAN finally meets Asgore, the ruler of the UNDERGROUND. No matter what happens, the king always dies because of Flowey or the HUMAN.

OMEGA FLOWEY

Once the King is dead, Flowey shows up again and shows his true form. Flowey has the ability to SAVE the game, just like the HUMAN. He is the final boss fight. At the end of the fight, the HUMAN has a choice: kill him, or spare him. Once a choice is made, the HUMAN can finally leave the UNDERGROUND.

SPECIAL SCREEN

If the HUMAN decided not to kill Flowey, a special screen will appear. In this screen, Flowey expresses his confusion as to why the HUMAN didn't kill him. Later, he tells the HUMAN to visit Alphys, as that is the last part to complete the True Pacifist Route.

TRUE LAB

After visiting Alphys and going on a date with her, the HUMAN discovers that Alphys has a secret lab, where it is revealed she was studying the SOUL power of HUMANS. Here, she accidently created Flowey, and she killed some of the test subjects. She is so ashamed of what she has done that she has hid away from everyone. At the end of the Lab, she decides to tell everyone the truth, thanking the HUMAN for helping her come to this realization.

THRONE ROOM & FLOWEY FIGHT

Once done with the TRUE LAB, the same events of the Throne Room will happen. This time however, Toriel will appear, stopping Asgore from attacking the HUMAN. Later on, everyone that the HUMAN has encountered throughout the game will appear, all thanks to Papyrus. Papyrus later reveals that a 'tiny flower' told him to reunite everyone. Flowey then uses this chance to attack everyone.

ASRIEL DREEMURR

It turns out, Flowey was Asriel Dreemurr all along. Asriel thinks that the HUMAN is Chara, the first HUMAN to fall down. However, it is revealed to us the true name of the HUMAN protagonist: Frisk. Frisk then reaches into Asriel's SOUL, saving each character one by one. In the end, they save Asriel, allowing him to be free at last. Frisk ends up passing out, and when they wake up, their friends are all there except Asriel.

THE TRUE END

In the end, Frisk breaks the barrier with their SOUL, freeing all of the monsters for once and for all. Asriel stays behind just in case another HUMAN falls down. This is where the TRUE PACIFIST Route ends.
