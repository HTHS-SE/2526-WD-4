NEUTRAL Timeline

To achieve this storyline, you cannot kill everyone (genocide run), or you must be on the pacifist route. This route is played by everyone who plays UNDERTALE, whether they know that there are different routes or not.


RUINS

When the HUMAN wakes up, they are greeted to Flowey the Flower. There, the HUMAN also encounter their first fight. A theme with Flowey is "Kill or be killed". When the HUMAN is at 1 HP, Toriel saves them by attacking Flowey. Toriel then shows the HUMAN around, acting as a caregiver to them. Toriel is also the first main battle that the HUMAN most fight.

SNOWDIN

Once the HUMAN leaves the RUINS, they then go into SNOWDIN town, home to Sans and Papyrus. Here, the HUMAN completes many puzzles for Papyrus. They are all relatively simple, so Papyrus decides to challenge the HUMAN by attacking them, making him he second boss fight the HUMAN encounters.

WATERFALL

After SNOWDIN, the HUMAN passes by WATERFALL, where they run away from Undyne, the Royal Guarde in UNDERTALE whose goal is to kill a HUMAN to get their soul for Asgore. If the HUMAN decides to spare Undyne, then she decides to not attack them anymore.

HOTLAND

When entering HOTLAND, the HUMAN meets Alphys in her lab. The HUMAN learns that Alphys has been stalking them the entire time, watching their every move. The HUMAN also meet Mettaton, a robot who is thristy for a HUMAN soul. While going through HOTLAND, the HUMAN has to complete various puzzles that Mettaton has set up.

MTT Resort

MTT RESTORT is where the HUMAN learns that Mettaton is a beloved figure in the UNDERGROUND, having a TV show dedicated to game shows. This is also where the HUMAN fights Mettaton. No matter what the HUMAN does, Mettaton will always explode.

TRUE HOME

Walking deeper into the UNDERGROUND, the HUMAN finds the TRUE HOME, which is an identical replica of the RUINS, just in black and white. This is where the HUMAN learns from all the monsters that they have met that Toriel and Asgore were married, and they had a child together. One day, a HUMAN feel into the underground, where they took them as their own. The HUMAN feel ill one day, and Asriel decided to bring their bdoy back up as it was their final wish. However, the humans above thought Asriel killed the HUMAN, so they killed Asriel. From then on, Asgore decided that any human that falls down must be killed. Because, Toriel divorced him. The TRUE HOME was their first home.

JUDGEMENT HALL

In the JUDGEMENT HALL, the HUMAN meets Sans again, but this time, he's more serious. Here, the HUMAN is judged for everything they have done. Whether they got any EXP (execution points), or their gained LOVE (level of violence), they are judged. Depending on the amount of kills the HUMAN has commited, Sans says different things. You don't fight Sans in the Neutral Timeline.

THRONE ROOM

This is the last room the HUMAN goes through. Here, the HUMAN finally meets Asgore, the ruler of the UNDERGROUND. No matter what happens, the king always dies because of Flowey or the HUMAN.

OMEGA FLOWEY

Once the King is dead, Flowey shows up again and shows his true form. Flowey has teh ability to SAVE the game, just like the HUMAN. He is the final boss fight. At the end of the fight, the HUMAN has a choice: kill him, or spare him. Once a choice is made, the HUMAN can finally leave the UNDERGROUND.

END
