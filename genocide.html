GENOCIDE Timeline

In the TRUE PACIFIST Timeline, you cannot kill anything. If you do, you are unable to complete the timeline. Throughout the game, you also have to go on dates with certain characters. Many people have also called this route the canon/true ending to UNDERTALE.


Since the Neutral Timeline still occurs during the Pacifist Run, we have included the Neutral timeline. There is some extra information on how to complete the route correctly. If you would like to skip the Neutral Timeline, please click this button.


RUINS

When the HUMAN wakes up, they are greeted to Flowey the Flower. There, the HUMAN also encounters their first fight. A theme with Flowey is "Kill or be killed." When the HUMAN is at 1 HP, Toriel saves them by attacking Flowey. Toriel then shows the HUMAN around, acting as a caregiver to them. Toriel is also the first main battle that the HUMAN must fight. It's very important to kill everyone in the RUINS. The moment the HUMAN gets the text 'But nobody came...', everyone in that section is dead. When it comes to Toriel's battle, the HUMAN kills her in one go.

SNOWDIN

Once the HUMAN leaves the RUINS, they then go into SNOWDIN town, home to Sans and Papyrus. Here, the HUMAN completes many puzzles for Papyrus. They are all relatively simple, so Papyrus decides to challenge the HUMAN by attacking them, making him the second boss fight the HUMAN encounters. Once again, the HUMAN must kill everyone in this section. Once Papyrus is killed in one hit, Sans won't appear again.

WATERFALL

After SNOWDIN, the HUMAN passes by WATERFALL, where they run away from Undyne, the Royal Guarde in UNDERTALE whose goal is to kill a HUMAN to get their soul for Asgore. Once again, the HUMAN must kill everyone in this section. Undyne unleashes her True Form, where her battle becomes signifantly more difficult. She ends up melting from too much DETERMINATION.

HOTLAND

When entering HOTLAND, the HUMAN meets Alphys in her lab. The HUMAN learns that Alphys has been stalking them the entire time, watching their every move. The HUMAN also meets Mettaton, a robot who is thristy for a HUMAN soul. Once again, the HUMAN must kill everyone in this section. All of the previous puzzles have already been solved due to the monsters running away.

MTT Resort

MTT RESTORT is where the HUMAN learns that Mettaton is a beloved figure in the UNDERGROUND who has a TV show dedicated to game shows. This is also where the HUMAN fights Mettaton. Mettaton also has a true form, Mettaton NEO. However, Mettaton dies in one hit.

TRUE HOME

Walking deeper into the UNDERGROUND, the HUMAN finds the TRUE HOME, which is an identical replica of the RUINS, just in black and white. This is where the HUMAN learns from Flowey that Toriel and Asgore were married, and they had a child together. One day, a HUMAN fell into the underground whom they took in as their own. The HUMAN fell ill one day, and Asriel decided to bring their body back up as it was their final wish. However, the humans above thought Asriel killed the HUMAN, so they killed Asriel. From then on, Asgore decided that any human who falls down must be killed. Therefore, Toriel divorced him. The TRUE HOME was their first home. At the end of the story, Flowey expresses that he is afraid of the HUMAN.

JUDGEMENT HALL

In the JUDGEMENT HALL, the HUMAN meets Sans again, but this time, he's more serious. Here, the HUMAN is judged for everything they have done. Sans is aware of what the HUMAN has done, and he is planning his revenge.

SANS BATTLE

After Sans's "Judgement" on the HUMAN, he decides to fight the HUMAN. Although he is the easiest compared to all other bosses, he is the toughest to defeat. The HUMAN has to wait until he falls asleep in order to kill him.

THRONE ROOM

After killing Sans, the HUMAN goes to Asgore's Throne Room, where he doesn't want to fight the HUMAN. Instead, he tries to invite the HUMAN to tea. The HUMAN, without the player doing anything, starts to kill him, with Flowey getting the finishing blow.

FLOWEYS DEATH

Once Asgore is dead, Flowey begs for any MERCY from the HUMAN. But, once again without the player doing anything, he dies.

CHARA

Once Flowey is dead, Chara, the first HUMAN to fall into the UNDERGROUND, appears. Here, they give the user a choice: erase the whole world or keep it. No matter what the user chooses, the world will erase. If the user puts do not erase, then Chara will jumpscare the user.

THE END

In the end, no matter what choice the user decides, the game ends with Chara killing the player. Then, the game closes. When trying to re-open the game, nothing will happen.

PACIFIST ENDING (AFTER GENOCIDE)

If the player decides to do a Pacifist run after Genocide, the ending screen will be different. No matter what, the game will always remember what the player has done.
