UNDERTALE Timelines

UNDERTALE has different timelines depending on the actions of the player. Although most events stay the same, the are other events that have drastic charges. There are three main routes that the fandom of UNDERTALE have named: the True Pacifist route, the Neutral Route, and the Genocide Route.


IN THE BEGINNING...

19XX

In the beginning, two races lived in harmony; Humans and Monsters. Up until then, there were no issues with the two races. At some point, however, the humans became worried that the monsters were going to use their SOULS to destroy them.

WAR

19XX

Because of the Human's fear, they decided to go to war. The Humans ended up winning, and they decided to banish the monsters UNDERGROUND a mountain with a special spell. According to the spell, only someone with a HUMAN SOUL can leave the UNDERGROUND, meaning the monsters can never leave.

YEARS LATER...

201X

Years later, a HUMAN decided to climb up the Mountain out of curiosity. They went up with nobody nobody else but themselves.

FALLING UNDERGROUND

201X

When at the top of the mountain, the HUMAN accidently tripped and fell into the mountain, falling head first into a bed of yellow flowers.

UNDERTALE

201X

This is where the story of UNDERTALE begins.
